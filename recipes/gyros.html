Gyros

Description

A greek favorite with a twist. Fresh and filling ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 10 pounds of joe rogan branded elk meat
  • pita bread
  • one cucumber
  • two tomatoes
  • tzatziki sauce

Steps

  1. Cook meat in oven
  2. Cut meat and place in pita
  3. Chop and add cucumber and tomatoes
  4. Drizzle tzatziki sauce and enjoy
